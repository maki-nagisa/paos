# Templates Roadmap

## Purpose
- Define the template surface for `PAOS` roadmap artifacts.

## Scope
- Register the template rules for roadmap initiatives and planning artifacts in `PAOS`.
- Keep structural planning outputs compact and architecture-facing.
- Do not let roadmap templates expand into task-log prose or execution diaries.

## Dependencies
- `templates/Templates-Module.md`
- `templates/roadmap/roadmap-template.md`

## Inputs
- Roadmap structure rules
- Initiative metadata rules

## Outputs
- Roadmap template registry
- Initiative creation anchor

## References
- `templates/roadmap/roadmap-template.md`
- `roadmap/Roadmap-Module.md`
- `roadmap/roadmap.index.md`

## Roadmap Template Role

Roadmap templates exist to keep future-change contracts structurally comparable.
They solve the recurring problem of initiatives drifting into narrative planning without explicit fields for target state, dependencies, and exit criteria.

## Registry Rule

- use `roadmap-template.md` when creating or normalizing initiative-style planning artifacts
- keep roadmap templates aligned with `roadmap/Roadmap-Module.md` so planning structure and planning governance stay consistent
- add new fields only when they materially improve recurring structural planning decisions

- `templates/roadmap/roadmap-template.md`
