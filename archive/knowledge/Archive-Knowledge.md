# Archive Knowledge

## Purpose
- Register retired or superseded knowledge artifacts that no longer belong in active context.

## Scope
- Register retired `knowledge` artifacts whose authority has been replaced or withdrawn.
- Keep historical knowledge discoverable by explicit lookup without restoring it to active authority.
- Do not become a mirror of the live `knowledge` tree.

## Dependencies
- `archive/Archive-Module.md`
- `knowledge/Knowledge-Module.md`

## Inputs
- Archived knowledge modules
- Replacement mappings

## Outputs
- Knowledge archive registry
- Archive lookup anchor

## References
- `archive/Archive-Module.md`
- `knowledge/Knowledge-Module.md`
- `core/conventions/references.md`

## Knowledge Archive Role

This registry exists to hold durable knowledge that is no longer active but still worth retaining for historical comparison or replacement tracing.

## Registry Rule

- archive knowledge only after a clearer current owner, retirement decision, or superseding contract exists
- preserve replacement mapping or retirement reason rather than copying large active knowledge blocks into archive
- keep this registry small and explicit; if nothing merits retention, keep the registry minimal
