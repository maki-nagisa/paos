# Archive Roadmap

## Purpose
- Register completed or retired roadmap artifacts that no longer belong to the active planning surface.

## Scope
- Register completed, superseded, or retired roadmap artifacts after their planning authority ends.
- Keep planning history reachable by explicit lookup without polluting the active roadmap surface.
- Do not become an execution transcript or planning diary.

## Dependencies
- `archive/Archive-Module.md`
- `roadmap/Roadmap-Module.md`

## Inputs
- Archived roadmap items
- Initiative replacement mappings

## Outputs
- Roadmap archive registry
- Archive lookup anchor

## References
- `archive/Archive-Module.md`
- `roadmap/Roadmap-Module.md`
- `roadmap/roadmap.index.md`

## Roadmap Archive Role

This registry exists to retain historical planning decisions after they stop governing future work.

## Registry Rule

- archive roadmap items only after their durable result is reflected into active canonical modules or the initiative is explicitly retired
- preserve closure or replacement meaning instead of replaying the full planning discussion
- keep active roadmap and archived roadmap clearly separated so old intent cannot masquerade as current intent
